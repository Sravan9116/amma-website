Amma and the Indian Knowledge System (IKS)

Amma's vision is deeply rooted in the Indian Knowledge System (IKS), emphasizing holistic education, compassion, and selfless service. Amma mission towards Bhajan align with the core principles of IKS, promoting harmony between humanity and nature.

Amma's Activities and Contributions

Amma's contributions span into various domains, reflecting her commitment to serve humanity:

  • Higher Educational Initiatives: Establishing Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, a multi-disciplinary research university consist of techinal education and medical arts law and so on.
  • Healthcare: Importing affordable and quality healthcare, Amrita Institute of Medical Sciences (AIMS), providing way to public.
  • Relief from Natural Calamities: Leading disaster relief efforts worldwide, providing immediate assistance and long-term rehabilitation.
  • Environmental Conservation: Implementing projects to promote environmental sustainability and raise awareness about climate change to preserve the planet.
  • Poverty Alleviation: Implementing programs towards women empowerment, provide vocational/hands on trainings, and support sustainable livelihoods.

Amma and the Indian Knowledge System (IKS)

Amma's vision is deeply rooted in the Indian Knowledge System (IKS), emphasizing holistic education, compassion, and selfless service. Her teachings align with the core principles of IKS, promoting harmony between humanity and nature.

Amma's Activities and Contributions

Amma's contributions span various domains, reflecting her commitment to serving humanity:

  • Educational Initiatives: Establishing Amrita Vishwa Vidyapeetham, a multi-campus, multi-disciplinary research university.
  • Healthcare: Founding Amrita Institute of Medical Sciences (AIMS), providing affordable and quality healthcare.
  • Disaster Relief: Leading disaster relief efforts worldwide, providing immediate assistance and long-term rehabilitation.
  • Environmental Conservation: Implementing projects to promote environmental sustainability and raise awareness about climate change.
  • Poverty Alleviation: Implementing programs to empower women, provide vocational training, and support sustainable livelihoods.

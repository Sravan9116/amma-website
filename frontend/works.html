Overview

Amma's humanitarian activities span healthcare, education, disaster relief, environmental initiatives, rural development, women empowerment, and technological innovation through the Amrita institutions and charities.

Healthcare

Hospitals & mobile clinics

Amrita Hospital and mobile clinics provide affordable and free healthcare, advanced surgeries, and outreach medical camps for the poor.

Free Surgeries

Specialized care

Thousands of heart surgeries, cancer treatments, and advanced procedures have been provided free of charge.

Education

Schools & universities

Over 60 primary and secondary schools, scholarships, and Amrita University provide quality education rooted in compassion and innovation.

Research & Scholarships

Empowering students

Amma’s institutions support global research in AI, robotics, biotechnology, and provide scholarships for thousands of rural students.

Disaster Relief

Immediate response

Amma’s organization provided large-scale relief during the 2004 tsunami, floods, and earthquakes, rebuilding homes and communities.

Flood & Earthquake Aid

Rehabilitation

Volunteers distribute food, medical help, and rebuild villages after disasters across India and abroad.

Women Empowerment

Self-help groups

Amma has organized over 30,000 self-help groups across India, providing microfinance, training, and livelihoods for women.

AmritaSREE

Vocational training

The AmritaSREE project empowers women with skill-building, small business support, and sustainable employment opportunities.

Environmental Care

Tree planting & conservation

Millions of trees have been planted under Amma’s Green India initiative, promoting sustainability and biodiversity.

Cleanliness Drives

Public awareness

Massive campaigns like “Clean India” inspired by Amma’s message have mobilized lakhs of volunteers to clean streets and rivers.

Technology for Humanity

Innovating for society

Amrita University research labs develop AI, robotics, and biomedical devices aimed at disaster relief and healthcare access.

Digital Learning

Online education

Amma’s vision supports e-learning platforms bringing high-quality education to rural villages through digital tools.
